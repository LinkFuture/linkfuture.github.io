Privacy Policy

At LinkFuture LLC, we respect and protect the privacy of all our users. We designed UniBucket with a Local-First approach, meaning we do not collect, process, or store your personal data, credentials, or cloud files on our servers.

Local Storage & Encryption

Your cloud provider credentials, such as AWS access keys or Google Cloud JSON files, are encrypted and strictly stored on your local machine. These sensitive pieces of information are never transmitted to any external tracking or telemetry systems maintained by LinkFuture.

Data Collection

The UniBucket application operates entirely on your device. All communications for file transfers occur directly between your machine and your chosen cloud providers (e.g., AWS, GCS). We have zero visibility into your files or object storage infrastructure.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about the local processing of your data, please contact us by creating an issue on our GitHub repository.
