About LinkFuture LLC

Located in the heart of the Pacific Northwest—Bothell, Washington—LinkFuture LLC is an independent software studio dedicated to building high-performance, developer-centric tools.

Our Mission

We believe that cloud management shouldn't feel like a chore. Our goal is to bridge the gap between complex cloud infrastructures and a seamless desktop experience. We build the tools we wanted to use ourselves: software that is fast, intuitive, and stays out of your way.

Security & Trust First

As developers, we understand that your cloud credentials are your "keys to the kingdom." That’s why we architect our flagship product, UniBucket, with a "Local-First" philosophy. Your keys never touch our servers; they stay encrypted on your machine, exactly where they belong.

When you choose a LinkFuture product, you're not just getting a tool—you're getting a commitment to privacy, performance, and professional-grade engineering.
